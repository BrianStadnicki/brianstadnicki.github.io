Welcome to Canopy, the spaced repetition revision tracker!
  • Every time you learn something (at school), create a new review for it.
  • Each day, check what you need to review.
  • Answer some questions about each topic for the day.
  • Mark your answers.
  • Mark the review as completed and enter your score.

That's it. Nothing complex like Anki flash cards.
No fanfare, no charts, no fluff, nothing to distract you.
Canopy is just a tracker.

If you find an issue or have a suggestion, contact Brian Stadnicki at brian.canopy.tracker@gmail.com
